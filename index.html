Health & Safety Guidelines

🏃

Exercise Wisely

Avoid outdoor exercise when AQI exceeds 100. Choose early morning hours when pollution levels are typically lower.

🪟

Ventilation Tips

Keep windows closed during high pollution periods. Use air purifiers indoors and ventilate during cleaner hours.

😷

Mask Protection

Wear N95 or N99 masks when AQI exceeds 150 to protect your respiratory system from harmful pollutants.

🌱

Indoor Greenery

Keep air-purifying plants like snake plants and peace lilies to naturally improve indoor air quality.

💧

Hydration Matters

Drink plenty of water to help your body flush out toxins and maintain healthy respiratory function.

📱

Stay Updated

Check air quality forecasts daily and plan outdoor activities based on pollution alerts.
